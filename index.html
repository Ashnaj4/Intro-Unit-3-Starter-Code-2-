The Horror Roadtrip

You and 3 other friends are on a roadtrip to the outskirts of upstate New York for a weekend trip ,when all of a sudden your car breaks down and there's no help around . You and your friends decide to walk until you guys found a creepy old house to ask for help but something alarms you .

While walking towards the house , 3 diguised figures with machetes come your way to attack , you and your friends run getting split up .

You meet back up with your friends and attempt to outrun the killer's.

You successfully outrun the killers and attempt to make it to the car
